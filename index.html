You Don't Need Hours — You Need to Add Value
Content Methods
Content doesn't have to be long. It has to be valuable.
01
Short Post
Even 100 words counts. Keep your profile active and your perspective visible.
02
Voice Recording
Brief script, record on your phone, transcribe, refine with AI. 30 minutes speaking generates thousands of words.
03
Short Video
30 seconds to 1 minute. Native video performs strongly on the platform. No studio required.
04
Infographic
Use data you already have. Visualise an insight your audience does not have access to.
05
Data Post
A single insight or stat with context. One number, well explained, is a complete post.

Pre-Writing
Checklist
Before You Write
Ask Yourself These
01
Who do I want to read this?
Think of 2–3 specific people. What do they do? What do they care about? What are their pain points?
02
What am I adding that is new?
A new perspective, stat, or challenge to a common assumption. If you cannot add something new, it is not worth writing.
03
Why should anyone care?
Add context. Hook it to what is happening in the world right now. Make it timely and relevant.
04
Would I actually read this?
Be honest. If the answer is no — start again. Do not post for the sake of posting.
05
How can I make this genuinely valuable?
Think: insights, data points, opportunities, edge. Content that gets saved and shared gives readers something they can actually use.
